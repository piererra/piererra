I'm Piererra, a developer focused on browser-based tools. My work began with reverse-engineering GTA San Andreas save files and has grown into a suite of editors used by players worldwide. Everything I build runs entirely client-side — free, open-source, and private by design.

ABOUT

Self-taught, detail-driven, and focused on games.

Piererra began as a personal project to repair my own save files and has since grown into a suite of editors used by a wider community of players. No save data is ever uploaded — every tool runs entirely in the browser, start to finish.

  1. Started modding GTA San Andreas
    Began by manually editing save files byte by byte, driven by curiosity and persistence.
  2. Taught myself development
    Built on that experience to learn full browser-based web development.
  3. Shipped Piererra
    Released four live editors, translated into 75+ languages and used by players around the world.
